Three attributes about heros

There are three attributes of the heros: strength, agility, and intelligence.

  • Power attributes

    Add a little bit of power to add 19 HP and 0.03 HP/SEC regeneration.

  • Agility attribute

    Increases the attack speed by 1% for every bit of agility, and adds a bit of armor every 7 points

  • Intellectual attribute

    :Increases the mana regeneration rate by 13 mana and 0.04 / SEC
